Beef Stew


A traditional english meal.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Potatoes
  • carrots
  • onion
  • 100ml stock cube

Method

  1. Cook beef in pan until medium rare
  2. Add vegatables to steamer
  3. Wait 30 minutes
  4. Add all ingredients to baking pot and place in oven at 200 degrees for 20 minutes
  5. Boil the potatoes for 40 minutes
