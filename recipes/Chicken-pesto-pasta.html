Chicken Pesto Pasta


A scrumptous and hearty chicken meal

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package bow tie pasta
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Â½ cup pesto sauce

Method

  1. Chop the chicken into chunks
  2. Cook them until juices run clear
  3. Marinate with pesto sauce
  4. Add the olive oil
